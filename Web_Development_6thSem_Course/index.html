The goal of Health PLUS is the attainment of the highest possible level of health.

The aim of this online platform is to redefine the way we look at hospitals and the health culture and to STRENGTHEN the relation between Doctors and Patients.

Through this, we believe to reach out to all those who are unable to afford an expensive hospital and are desperate to get treated.
